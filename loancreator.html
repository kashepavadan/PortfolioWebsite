LoanCreator

  • Below you'll find a simple loan protocol for Ether. To borrow money, simply submit a "Loan Request" below, and specify your desired loan interest rate, term, and ETH value.

    Then, a third party can fund your loan request by submitting the loan amount plus interest and become your loan's guarantor. At this point, you (the borrower) will receive funds based on the loan request.

    You can repay the loan all at once or in installments, but make sure to pay it off on time!

    The guarantor of the loan receives the proportion of the loan (with interest) that's paid off on time, as well as a reward equal to the proportional interest value. Your credit score will increase, improving your chances of having your loan requests funded in the future.

    If the loan expires without being repaid, your credit score will decrease.

    To encourage the removal of an expired loan, liquidators are compensated with a reward equal to the guarantor's deposited interest value.

    IMPORTANT! You must connect to the Sepolia testnet only!
